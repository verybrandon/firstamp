The Great White Boy Hip Hop War of 2018

It all started when Eminem surprise released his new album Kamikaze. On the album he disses fellow white rapper, Machine Gun Kelly.

"But next time you don't gotta use Tech N9ne
If you wanna come at me with a sub, Machine Gun
And I'm talkin' to you, but you already know who the fuck you are, Kelly"

This comes from constant feuding between the two, that started from MGK saying Eminem's daughter looked hot when she was only 16.

A couple of days afterwards, Machine Gun Kelly comes out with a full track dissing the rap god himself called "Rap Devil".

The next day G-Eazy releases a diss track towards MGK called "Bad Boy". This comes on the back of MGK dissing G-Eazy in a freestyle rap in a radio interview.

Will anyone else join the great war? What sides will people fall on? Who will get dissed next?
